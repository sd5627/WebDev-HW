# 🍝 Garlic Butter Pasta

## Description
A quick and comforting pasta dish made with garlic, butter, and parmesan. Perfect for busy nights.

---

## 📝 Ingredients
- 200g pasta
- 3 cloves garlic
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Parsley (optional)

---

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Cook pasta until al dente.
3. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
4. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
5. Toss pasta into the pan.
6. Add parmesan, salt, and pepper.
7. Garnish with parsley and serve.

---

## ⭐ Notes
- Add chili flakes for heat  
- Works well with shrimp or mushrooms
