Simple Lemon Pasta

A bright, fresh pasta dish that comes together in under 30 minutes.

Servings 2
Prep 10 mins
Cook 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Lemon zest
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil and sauté garlic.
  3. Add lemon zest and cooked pasta.
  4. Finish with parmesan and serve.
