Truffle Cream Mushroom Pasta

松露奶油蘑菇培根通心粉 — Creamy truffle mushroom pasta with bacon.

Servings 1
Prep 10 mins
Cook 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 porcini mushrooms
  • 1 slice bacon
  • 120 g penne pasta
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 g truffle sauce
  • 65 g light cream
  • 12 g olive oil
  • 3 g salt
  • 2 g black pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Slice the mushrooms and bacon.
  2. Chop the onion and mince the garlic.
  3. Cook penne pasta in boiling water for about 7 minutes. Drain and toss with olive oil.
  4. Sauté mushrooms, bacon, onion, and garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  5. Add cream and truffle sauce, then stir well.
  6. Add pasta and mix evenly.
  7. Serve on a plate and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
