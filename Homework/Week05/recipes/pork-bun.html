Steamed Pork Bun

鲜肉包 — Fluffy steamed buns filled with savory pork.

Servings 4
Prep 1 hr
Cook 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 300 g ground pork
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger and scallions

Directions

  1. Mix ground pork with soy sauce, ginger, and scallions.
  2. Prepare the dough with flour and yeast and let it rise.
  3. Divide the dough and wrap the pork filling inside.
  4. Place buns in a steamer and steam for about 15 minutes.
