British Big Breakfast

英式全餐 — A classic hearty English breakfast plate.

Servings 1
Prep 15 mins
Cook 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 20 ml light cream
  • 2 slices French bread
  • 2 breakfast sausages
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 46 g baked beans
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Spread butter on the French bread and bake in a 180°C oven for 5 minutes.
  2. Slice the avocado and set aside.
  3. Place the sausages in cold water. Bring to a boil, then turn off heat and rest for 3 minutes.
  4. Pan-fry bacon and sausages until golden.
  5. Sauté tomato and mushrooms with salt and pepper.
  6. Whisk eggs with cream and cook slowly with butter to make soft scrambled eggs.
  7. Arrange everything on a plate and serve warm.
