Century Egg Congee

皮蛋瘦肉粥 — Classic comforting rice porridge.

Servings 2
Prep 15 mins
Cook 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 100 g minced pork
  • 1 century egg
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Boil rice with water until the porridge becomes thick and creamy.
  2. Add minced pork and cook for about 5 minutes.
  3. Dice the century egg and stir it into the congee.
  4. Season with salt and serve hot.
