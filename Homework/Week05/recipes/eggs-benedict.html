Eggs Benedict

班尼迪克蛋 — Classic poached eggs with hollandaise sauce.

Servings 1
Prep 15 mins
Cook 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 English muffin
  • 2 slices smoked salmon
  • 75 g spinach
  • 5 g lemon juice
  • 50 g butter
  • 65 ml white vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Slice the English muffin in half, spread butter, and toast until golden.
  2. Bring water to a boil, add vinegar, and reduce to a simmer. Poach eggs for about 3 minutes.
  3. Sauté spinach with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Prepare hollandaise sauce by whisking egg yolks and lemon juice over warm water, slowly adding melted butter.
  5. Assemble the dish: muffin base, spinach, smoked salmon, poached eggs, and drizzle with hollandaise sauce.
