Spicy Mussel Seafood Pasta

青口贝辣海鲜意面 — Spicy seafood pasta with mussels and shrimp.

Servings 1
Prep 10 mins
Cook 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 mussels
  • 2 black tiger shrimps
  • 4 clams
  • 4 squid rings
  • 120 g spaghetti
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • White wine
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Peel shrimp and season the seafood with salt, pepper, and white wine. Marinate for about 5 minutes.
  2. Slice the garlic and chop the Parmesan cheese.
  3. Cook spaghetti in boiling water for about 5–7 minutes.
  4. Sauté garlic, seafood, and tomato sauce in olive oil.
  5. Add tomatoes and pasta, then simmer until the seafood opens and the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt, black pepper, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Plate the pasta and arrange the seafood on top.
