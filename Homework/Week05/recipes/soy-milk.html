Fresh Soy Milk

现磨豆浆 — Traditional homemade soybean milk.

Servings 2
Prep 8 hrs soak
Cook 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried soybeans
  • 6 cups water
  • Sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Soak soybeans in water overnight.
  2. Blend the soaked soybeans with fresh water until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture through cheesecloth to remove solids.
  4. Bring the soy milk to a boil and cook for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Add sugar if desired and serve warm.
