Scallion Pancake

葱油饼 — Crispy layered Chinese flatbread.

Servings 3
Prep 30 mins
Cook 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup hot water
  • 2–3 scallions, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for dough layers)
  • 2–3 tablespoons oil for pan frying

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour with hot water slowly. Stir with chopsticks or a spoon until the dough starts to form.
  2. Knead the dough on a clean surface for about 5 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rest for about 20 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into 3 equal pieces and roll each piece into a thin circle.
  4. Brush a thin layer of oil on the surface, then sprinkle with salt and chopped scallions.
  5. Roll the dough into a long log, then coil it into a spiral shape and flatten gently.
  6. Roll the spiral again into a round pancake about 6–7 inches wide.
  7. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a small amount of oil.
  8. Cook the pancake for about 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove from the pan, cut into wedges, and serve warm.
