Youtiao

油条 — Traditional Chinese deep-fried dough stick.

Servings 2
Prep 20 mins
Cook 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • Oil for deep frying

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add water and oil, then knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let the dough rest for about 20 minutes.
  4. Roll the dough flat and cut into long strips.
  5. Stack two strips together and press lightly in the middle.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the dough sticks until golden and crispy.
  7. Turn occasionally while frying to cook evenly.
  8. Remove and drain on paper towels before serving.
