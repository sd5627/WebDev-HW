About Shihan's Kitchen

Shihan’s Kitchen is a personal recipe collection inspired by traditional Chinese home cooking and simple everyday meals. This site is a space to share comfort food, family favorites, and easy recipes that anyone can recreate.

Cooking has always been about connection — to culture, to memory, and to the people we share meals with. From classic breakfast dishes like soy milk and scallion pancakes to modern pasta creations, each recipe reflects warmth and simplicity.

Thank you for visiting and exploring these recipes. I hope they bring joy to your kitchen.
